WHO WE ARE

COMPASS is a multi-tiered medical research foundation that serves people’s mental health and wellbeing through accelerated access to innovative, evidence-based, personalized care models. We support innovation in mental health through translational research.

WHY WE EXIST

Most emotional and mental suffering is part of human life. If not recognised and supported, it may become a medical diagnosis or carry a stigma of psychopathology.

We believe that the path to mental health is unique for each person and that individuals benefit most when they are actively involved in the design of their own care pathways.

We believe in a fundamental human right to choose one’s own path through extremes of suffering to flourishing..

HOW WE HELP

Translational research is an increasingly recognized innovative paradigm of translating basic science and clinical trials data into knowledge and prac tice, creating new care pathways. Translational research requires active engagement of all members of the “ecosystem”: scientists, clinicians, patients, regulators and payers, throughout the entire process.
